Berber Quran — القرآن الكريم الأمازيغي

📚 The first full-platform application to offer a complete and authenticated translation of the Holy Quran into the Amazigh (Kabyle) language, based on trusted and certified sources from the King Fahd Complex for the Printing of the Holy Quran. Available on Web, Android, and iOS — completely free and ad-free.

This project is the cross-platform evolution of the original "Holy Quran Tamazight" Android app, now rebuilt using Flutter to deliver a unified experience across all devices. It includes expert commentaries, audio recitations, Amazigh audio translations, daily supplications, Hijri calendar, a digital Tafsir library, and much more.

Key Features:


  •  🌟 Authenticated Amazigh Translation: A precise and complete translation of the Holy Quran meanings in Amazigh (Kabyle), based on trusted sources.
  •  📜 Expert Commentaries (Tafsir): Verified interpretations from specialized scholars with simplified explanations for each verse.
  •  🔍 Advanced Search: Smart search engine for instant access to any verse, surah, or word in the Quran.
  •  🎧 Audio Recitations: Listen to Quran recitation with your favorite reciter, with the ability to navigate between surahs.
  •  🗣️ Amazigh Audio Translation: A unique feature that lets you listen to the Amazigh translation for each verse audio-wise.
  •  ⭐ Favorites & Bookmarks: Manage your favorite verses and page bookmarks for a personalized reading experience.

Advanced Features:


  •  📿 Adhkar & Du'as: A comprehensive collection of morning and evening supplications with favorites and sharing support.
  •  📅 Hijri Calendar: Full Hijri calendar with religious occasions, countdowns, and holiday reminders.
  •  📚 Digital Tafsir Library: Downloadable Tafsir books with page-by-page reading, advanced search, and bookmarks.
  •  📖 Khatma Plan: Create a Quran completion plan and track your daily progress with the ability to create multiple khatmas.
  •  🔤 Waqf Signs: Learn Quranic stop signs with detailed explanations in multiple languages for mastering proper recitation.
  •  🎨 Share & Customize: Share verses as text or image, customize background colors, font size, and light/dark mode.
  •  🌐 Multi-Language: Available in three languages for a personalized experience.
  •  🌗 Light & Dark Themes: Comfortable reading in both day and night modes.

The app is still being updated — if you encounter any issues, please report them. Your feedback helps us improve! May it be a Sadaqah Jariyah (ongoing charity) for everyone who shares it.

Platforms
