Moultaqa al-Mawahib

Moultaqa al-Mawahib is a mobile application designed to showcase artistic talents and facilitate opportunities in the arts and events industry. The platform centralizes offerings, requests, training programs, and career management tools to create a comprehensive ecosystem for artists, trainers, and recruiters in the cultural sector.

Key Features:


  •   Subscription-Based Access System: Tiered subscription model providing access to specialized sections including offers, requests, and professional training resources.
  •   Talent Opportunity Platform: Dedicated spaces for publishing and discovering artistic opportunities, event offers, and specific talent requests within the community.
  •   Professional Development Hub: Comprehensive training section offering career management tools and professional development resources for artistic growth.
  •   Secure Authentication System: Robust login and registration pages ensuring data protection and user privacy within the artistic community.
  •   Integrated Payment Solutions: Multiple payment options including Dahabiya and Baridimob integration for seamless subscription management and access to premium features.

Advanced Features:


  •   Centralized Administration Dashboard: Comprehensive management interface for overseeing users, offers, requests, and payment processing across the platform.
  •   Artistic Community Network: Homepage featuring artistic journals and user activity feeds to foster community engagement and talent discovery.
  •   Career Management Tools: Specialized workspace for artists to manage their professional development, track opportunities, and plan career progression.
  •   Mission-Oriented Platform: Dedicated about section explaining the application's purpose, target audience, and commitment to supporting artistic talent development.

Moultaqa al-Mawahib creates a secure digital space that empowers artists by providing structured access to professional opportunities while facilitating meaningful connections between talent, trainers, and industry recruiters.

The platform addresses the unique needs of the artistic community by combining opportunity discovery with career management tools in a single, cohesive ecosystem designed for continuous professional growth.
