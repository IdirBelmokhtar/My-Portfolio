ICMMT'2025

The 4th edition of ICMMT 2025 is an international scientific conference organized by the Faculty of Technology at the University of Boumerdes. This prestigious event aims to foster exchanges between researchers, industry professionals, and socio-economic stakeholders around themes related to materials, mechanics, and innovative technologies. The developed web platform provides comprehensive conference management including abstract submission, registration handling, program access, and organizer dashboard functionality.

Key Features:


  •   Abstract Submission System: Complete online interface for researchers to submit and manage their abstracts with proper formatting and deadline tracking.
  •   Participant Registration Portal: Streamlined registration and online payment system categorized by participant type (student, academic, industry professional).
  •   Conference Presentation Platform: Comprehensive showcase of conference themes, scientific committee, and event information for international audience engagement.
  •   Pricing Integration: Flexible fee structure supporting both online and in-person participation modes with appropriate rate differentiation.
  •   Practical Information Hub: Complete calendar, payment procedures, contact information, and social program details for participant convenience.

Advanced Features:


  •   Centralized Administration Dashboard: Comprehensive management interface for handling abstracts, participants, payments, and automated notifications.
  •   Role-Based Access Control: Differentiated access levels for participants, administrators, and scientific committee members with appropriate permissions.
  •   Publication Management System: Complete workflow for conference proceedings including book of abstracts and Scopus-indexed chapter management.
  •   International Multilingual Interface: Primary English interface optimized for global academic audience with responsive design across all devices.

ICMMT'2025 platform represents a sophisticated academic management system designed to support international scientific collaboration while maintaining the highest standards of academic integrity and organizational efficiency.

The platform facilitates seamless interaction between the global research community and conference organizers, ensuring smooth operations from abstract submission through to publication of proceedings.

Access the ICMMT'2025 conference platform to submit abstracts, register for participation, or explore conference programs and scientific committees. The platform supports the entire conference lifecycle.
