Quick LTE Force (4G-5G)

"Quick LTE Force (4G-5G)" is a mobile application designed for managing network settings, aimed at users looking to optimize their 4G and 5G connections. The app helps you to force your phone to work on 4G or 5G only, preventing automatic switching between different network types. This ensures that your phone remains on 4G or 5G, even if the signal quality is poor.

Designed to offer an intuitive and responsive user interface, "Quick LTE Force" uses advanced technologies to manage app data and settings. The goal is to provide users with a powerful tool to enhance their network experience and maintain consistent connectivity.

With features such as network diagnostics and optimization recommendations, the app aims to deliver better management of mobile connections by preventing your phone from switching to other network modes when 4G LTE signal strength is weak.
