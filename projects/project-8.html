Schedule Time

"Schedule Time" is a mobile application designed to help you organize your time effectively by taking notes on your daily activities.

The app sends notifications to remind you of your daily tasks and synchronizes data with Firebase for seamless access across devices.

Features include the ability to add notes, set bookmarks, and receive task reminders, making it an essential tool for staying on top of your schedule.
