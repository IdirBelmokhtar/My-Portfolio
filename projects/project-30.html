PHARMACI

PHARMACI is a comprehensive digital health platform developed to facilitate access to medications and on-duty pharmacies in Algeria. The platform enables patients to easily locate available pharmacies and verify prescription availability, while providing pharmacies with tools to manage inventory, services, and online stores. This project modernizes the healthcare experience for patients, pharmacists, and para-pharmaceutical product vendors.

Key Features:


  •   Medication Search System: Prescription upload functionality with real-time availability verification to ensure medication accessibility.
  •   Interactive Pharmacy Map: Comprehensive location services for on-duty pharmacies during nights, weekends, and holidays for emergency medical needs.
  •   Pharmacy Management Dashboard: Complete interface for pharmacies to respond to prescriptions, manage inventory levels, and activate on-duty status.
  •   Virtual Store Platform: Multi-tier subscription system (Bronze, Silver, Gold) allowing vendors to create stores and publish products based on their package level.
  •   Dynamic Notification System: Real-time alerts for patients regarding prescription status and important pharmacy updates.

Advanced Features:


  •   Internal Messaging: Direct communication channel between patients and pharmacists for consultations and clarifications.
  •   Advertising Platform: Strategic placement for promotional banners throughout the platform to support business growth.
  •   Centralized Administration: Comprehensive admin dashboard for managing users, stores, inventory, subscription packages, prescriptions, and platform analytics.
  •   Role-Based Access Control: Clear separation and specialized interfaces for patients, pharmacists, and vendors with appropriate permissions.

PHARMACI represents a significant advancement in healthcare digitalization for Algeria, creating an integrated ecosystem that connects patients with healthcare providers while streamlining pharmacy operations and expanding market reach for vendors.

The platform addresses critical healthcare accessibility challenges while providing business growth opportunities for pharmacies and vendors through its comprehensive feature set and user-friendly design.
