Khadra DZ

Khadra DZ is a mobile application specializing in the sale of organic cosmetic products, connecting users with certified and non-certified producers. The project focuses on ensuring technical continuity following service provider transitions while enhancing user experience and expanding platform capabilities to support both producers and iOS users, with a strong emphasis on promoting BioCert certification for producers.

Key Features:


  •   Streamlined User Registration: Simplified sign-up process requiring only essential fields - Name, Surname, Email, and Phone Number for improved conversion rates.
  •   Enhanced Admin Application: Redesigned user interface with real-time analytics for sales, user activity, and producer performance monitoring.
  •   Producer Management Platform: Dedicated application for producers to manage products, process orders, handle payments, and communicate with customers.
  •   BioCert Certification Section: Comprehensive information portal detailing organic certification benefits, acquisition steps, and advantages for producers.
  •   Technical Continuity Assurance: Complete codebase maintenance and optimization following service provider transition with performance and security enhancements.

Advanced Features:


  •   Cross-Platform Expansion Analysis: Comprehensive feasibility study for iOS application development including security assessment and performance optimization strategies.
  •   Real-Time Analytics Dashboard: Advanced reporting tools providing insights into sales trends, user engagement, and producer activity metrics.
  •   Customer Notification System: Integrated communication platform enabling producers to send updates and order status notifications to customers.
  •   Product Management Tools: Complete inventory control system for producers with categorization, pricing, and availability management capabilities.

Khadra DZ represents a specialized e-commerce solution for the organic cosmetics market, bridging the gap between conscious consumers and quality-focused producers while promoting sustainable business practices through certification incentives.

The platform successfully maintains technical stability through provider transitions while continuously enhancing features to support business growth and user satisfaction in the organic products sector.

Access Platform

Access the Khadra DZ platform through our website or download the mobile application from Google Play to explore organic cosmetic products and connect with certified producers.
