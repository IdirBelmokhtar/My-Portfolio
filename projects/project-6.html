GDSC University of Boumerdes

An application designed to facilitate requests and export forms as PDFs for the Google Developer Student Club at the University of Boumerdes. It includes features that allow members to easily submit requests and manage related activities.

The app is built with Java for Android, ensuring efficient management and user engagement, enhancing the overall experience for members.

This application aims to streamline the club's operations and provide valuable resources to its members. For more information about GDSC, visit the official website.
